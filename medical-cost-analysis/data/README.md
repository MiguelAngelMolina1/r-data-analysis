# Data

This folder contains the dataset used in the **Medical Cost and Lifestyle Analysis** project.

## Dataset Source

The dataset was obtained from Kaggle:

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mirichoi0218/insurance

It contains information on **1,338 individuals** and their associated medical insurance costs.

## Dataset Description

The dataset is commonly used for statistical modeling and machine learning tasks related to healthcare cost prediction. It includes demographic characteristics and lifestyle variables that may influence medical expenses.

## Variables

The dataset includes the following variables:

- **age**  
  Age of the primary insurance beneficiary.

- **sex**  
  Gender of the insurance policy holder (male or female).

- **bmi**  
  Body Mass Index, a measure of body fat based on height and weight.

- **children**  
  Number of dependents covered by the insurance plan.

- **smoker**  
  Indicates whether the individual is a smoker (yes or no).

- **region**  
  Geographic region of residence in the United States (northeast, northwest, southeast, southwest).

- **charges**  
  Individual medical insurance costs billed by the health insurance provider.

## Notes

The dataset is used for educational and analytical purposes in this project. Any preprocessing, transformation, or modeling steps applied to the data are performed in the `scripts` directory.
